About

Researcher specializing in Computer-Aided Drug Design (CADD), working at the intersection of structural biology, cheminformatics, and machine learning.

My work spans the full CADD pipeline — from hit identification via virtual screening and molecular docking, to lead optimization through MD simulations and free energy calculations. I build automated pipelines that connect wet-lab hypotheses with computational predictions.

I'm particularly interested in leveraging deep learning to accelerate early-stage drug discovery, including structure-based approaches and chemical space exploration.
